# Trading Platforms

Your trading platform connects you to the market. It's where execution happens. Choose wisely.

---

## Top Platforms by Market

### Interactive Brokers (IBKR)

**Best for:** All markets, global access

| Feature | Value |
|---------|-------|
| Markets | Stock, forex, futures, options, crypto |
| Fees | Low |
| Executio | Excellent |
| Global | 200+ markets |

**Pros:**
- Global access
- Low fees
- Professional tools

**Cons:**
- Complex interface
- High learning curve

---

## Common Mistakes

### 1. Wrong Platform for Market

**Mistake:** Using crypto platform for stocks.

**Result:** Limited assets, bad execution.

**Fix:** Match platform to market.

---

### 2. Ignoring Fees

**Mistake:** Just looking at spreads.

**Result:** Hidden costs add up.

**Fix:** Check all-in fees.

---

### 3. No Test Before Real

**Mistake:** Trading real money immediately.

**Result:** Learning on expensive mistakes.

**Fix:** Paper trade first.

---

# Key Takeaways

1. **Match platform to market**
2. **Check fees all-in**
3. **Test before trading real**
4. **IBKR for global**
5. **OANDA for forex**
