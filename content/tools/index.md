# Common Mistakes

## 1. Indicator Overload

**Problem:** Adding too many indicators creates conflict.

**Result:**
- Bullish signal from RSI
- Bearish signal from MACD
- No idea what to do

**Fix:** Use 2-3 indicators max. Each should serve a purpose.

---

## 2. Blind Usage

**Problem:** Using indicators without understanding how they work.

**Result:** Blind faith leads to blind losses.

**Fix:** Understand the math before trading the signal.

---

## 3. Lagging Indicators

**Problem:** Using only lagging indicators for entries.

**Result:** Always entering late.

**Fix:** Combine with leading indicators or price action.

---

## 4. Analysis Paralysis

**Problem:** Too many tools creates confusion.

**Result:** No trade executed.

**Fix:** Pick one setup. Master it. Then expand.

---

## 5. Not Adjusting for Conditions

**Problem:** Using same tools in all market conditions.

**Result:**
- RSI useless in trending markets
- Bollinger bands useless in chop

**Fix:** Adjust tools to market state.

# Tool Selection Framework

## Step 1: Define Your Strategy

What are you trading?
- Trend following → MA, MACD
- Mean reversion → RSI, Bollinger
- Breakout → Volume, Pivots

---

## Step 2: Choose Core Indicators

Pick 2-3 that match your strategy:
- Trend: SMA/EMA
- Momentum: RSI
- Confirmation: Volume

---

## Step 3: Add Context

What's the broader market doing?
- Use higher timeframe for context
- Use key levels for confluence

---

## Step 4: Execute

Your tools are set. Now act.
