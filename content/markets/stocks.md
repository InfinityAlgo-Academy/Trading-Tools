# Stock Markets

The stock market is where companies raise capital and investors build wealth. It's the most studied market, with rich fundamentals and clear drivers.

---

## What Are Stocks?

Stocks represent ownership in a company. When you buy a stock, you own a piece of that company.

**Key features:**
- Company ownership
- Earnings-driven prices
- Fundamental analysis
- Exchange-traded

## Volatility Profile

### Typical Movement

| Asset Type | Daily Range % |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Large Cap | 1-3% |
| Mid Cap | 2-5% |
| Small Cap | 3-8% |
| Penny Stocks | 10%+ |

### Volatility Characteristics

- **Moderate volatility** — More stable than crypto
- **Earnings moves** — Big swings on reports
- **Sector rotation** — Money flows between sectors
- **News-driven** — Catalysts on headlines

## Best Strategies for Stocks

### 1. Trend Following

**Why it works:** Strong stocks trend upward

| Setup | Timeframe | Stop |
|-------|-----------|------|
| Breakout | Daily | 8% trail |
| Earnings beat | Daily | Below breakout |

### 2. Swing Trading

**Why it works:** Medium-term swings

| Setup | Timeframe | Target |
|-------|-----------|--------|
| Pullback to MA | 4-Hour | Previous high |

### 3. Earnings Play

**Why it works:** Earnings = big moves

| Setup | Timeframe | Strategy |
|-------|-----------|----------|
| Pre-earnings | Daily | Straddle setup |
| Post-earnings | Daily | Gap trade |

### 4. Growth Investing

**Why it works:** Winner stocks keep winning

| Setup | Timeframe | Criteria |
|-------|-----------|----------|
| Revenue growth | Weekly | >20% YoY |
| Improving margins | Weekly | Trend up |

## Stock Selection Criteria

### Fundamental

- **Revenue growth:** >15% YoY
- **Earnings beats:** Consistent beats
- **Profit margins:** Expanding
- **Free cash flow:** Positive

### Technical

- **Uptrend:** Higher highs/lows
- **Volume:** Increasing on breakouts
- **RS:** Relative strength vs. market

---

## Trading Tips

### Do

- Research before buying
- Start with large caps
- Use limit orders
- Set stop-losses

### Don't

- Buy unknown penny stocks
- Trade pre/post market (wide spreads)
- Ignore earnings dates
- Over-leverage
