# Financial Markets

Before you can trade, you must understand what you're trading. Each market has unique characteristics, behaviors, and opportunities.

This section covers every major financial market — so you can choose the right one for your personality, capital, and goals.

# Market Types

## [[markets/forex]]

The foreign exchange market — trading currency pairs.

- **Daily volume:** $7+ trillion
- **Hours:** 24/5
- **Best for:** Trend following, carry trading
- **Characteristics:** High liquidity, low commissions

- **Hours:** 24/7

## Market Personalities

### Forex

- **Personality:** Steady, long-term trends
- **Best traders:** Patient, systematic
- **Worst traders:** Impulsive, over-traders

**Why:** Large, liquid, driven by macro factors

---

### Crypto

- **Personality:** Volatile, 24/7
- **Best traders:** Risk-takers, flexible
- **Worst traders:** Risk-averse, sleep-dependent

**Why:** Extreme price swings, no market hours

---

### Stocks

- **Personality:** Fundamental, earnings-driven
- **Best traders:** Analytical, patient
- **Worst traders:** impatient, short-term

**Why:** Company performance drives price

---

### Indices

- **Personality:** Broad, directional
- **Best traders:** Macro-aware, patient
- **Worst traders:** News-reactive

**Why:** Reflects economy/market sentiment

---

### Commodities

- **Personality:** Supply/demand driven
- **Best traders:** Fundamental analysts
- **Worst traders:** Technical-only

**Why:** Physical markets, unique drivers

# Choosing Your Market

## Questions to Ask

1. **How much capital do you have?** → More = more markets accessible
2. **How much time do you have?** → 24/7 markets need monitoring
3. **What's your risk tolerance?** → Higher = crypto; Lower = indices
4. **Do you prefer fundamentals or technicals?** → Stocks = fundamentals; Forex = technicals
5. **Do you need sleep?** → Avoid 24/7 markets

---

## Market Selection Framework

| If You Want... | Choose... |
|----------------|-----------|
| Steady trends | Forex, Indices |
| High volatility | Crypto, Commodities |
| 24/7 trading | Forex, Crypto |
| Fundamental analysis | Stocks, Commodities |
| Low barriers | Forex, Crypto, Indices |
| High leverage | Forex, Crypto, Indices |
