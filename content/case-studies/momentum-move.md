# Momentum Move

Catching strong trends. Riding momentum.

---

## Momentum Characteristics

| Sign | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| Strong trend | HH/HL |
| Volume | High |
| Pullbacks | Shallow |
| Continuation | Strong |

---

## Momentum Trading

### Entry

```
Trend: Strong up
Pullback: Shallow
Entry: On bounce
Stop: Below pullback low
```

### Exit

```
Exit: Lower low breaks
Or: Target reached
Or: Trail stop
```

---

# Key Takeaways

1. **Strong trends** — HH/HL intact
2. **Shallow pullbacks** — With trend
3. **Trail stop** — Let profits run
