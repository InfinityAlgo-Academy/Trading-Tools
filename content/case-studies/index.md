# Case Study Structure

Each case study includes:

```
1. Setup - What was the setup
2. Entry - When/where entered
3. Market - What was happening
4. Outcome - Win/Loss
5. Analysis - Why it happened
6. Lesson - What to learn
```

---

# Learning System

## From Wins

1. What worked?
2. Why did setup work?
3. Can I replicate?
4. What's my takeaway?

## From Losses

1. What failed?
2. Why did it fail?
3. What should I do differently?
4. What's my takeaway?

## From Every Trade

1. Did I follow rules?
2. Was emotion involved?
3. What's one adjustment?
4. What's working?
