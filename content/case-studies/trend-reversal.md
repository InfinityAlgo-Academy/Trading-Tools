# Trend Reversal

Identifying trend reversals. Recognizing tops and bottoms.

---

## Reversal Signs

### Bearish Reversal

| Sign | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| Lower high | Momentum shifting |
| Divergence | Bearish signal |
| Volume spike | Exhaustion |
| Rejection candle | Rejection |

### Bullish Reversal

| Sign | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| Higher low | Support |
| Divergence | Bullish |
| Volume increase | Accumulation |
| Hammer/bottom | Reversal candle |
