# Success Patterns

What works. Analyze successful trades and patterns.

---

## Pattern 1: Trend Continuation Pullback

### Setup

```
Trend: Uptrend
Entry: Pullback to moving average
Confirmation: Price bounces
Result: +100 pips
```

### Why It Works

- With trend
- Good risk/reward
- Clear entry

---

## Pattern 2: Range Breakout

### Setup

```
Range: Tightening
Break: Strong volume
Entry: On break confirmation
Result: +80 pips
```

### Why It Works

- Volume confirms
- Follows structure
- Clear entry/exit

---

## Pattern 3: RSI Divergence

### Setup

```
Price: Lower low
RSI: Higher low
Entry: On trendline break
Result: +150 pips
```

### Why It Works

- Divergence = potential reversal
- Confirmation before entry
- Good location

---

## Pattern 4: Support Bounce

### Setup

```
Price: Tests support
Reaction: Bounces
Entry: On bounce confirmation
Result: +60 pips
```

### Why It Works

- Support holds
- Clear stop location
- Good R:R

---

## Pattern 5: Trendline Breakout

### Setup

```
Trendline: Uptrend line
Break: Breaks above
Entry: Retest holds
Result: +120 pips
```

### Why It Works

- Clear level
- Retest confirms
- Good R:R

---

# Key Takeaways

1. **With trend** — Continuation works
2. **Confirmation** — Wait for it
3. **Clear stops** — Know where out
4. **Good R:R** — Minimum 2:1
