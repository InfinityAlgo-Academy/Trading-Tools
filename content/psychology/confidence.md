# Confidence

Unwavering confidence comes from consistent execution and documented results.

---

## Building Confidence

### 1. Document Success

Track wins. Review them.

### 2. Follow Your Rules

Know you've done it right.

### 3. Accept Variance

Statistical edge works over time.

---

## Confidence Killers

| Killer | Cause |
|--------|-------|
| Recent loss | Recency bias |
| No journal | No proof |
| Broken rules | Inconsistency |

---

## Confidence Builders

| Builder | Use |
|---------|-----|
| Good process | Focus on this |
| Past wins | Remember them |
| Clear rules | Follow them |

---

# Key Takeaways

1. **Document results** — Build proof
2. **Follow rules** — Build consistency
3. **Accept variance** — Mathematical edge
4. **Focus on process** — Not outcome
