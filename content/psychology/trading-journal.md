# Trading Journal

A trading journal is your growth tool. Track everything, learn from everything.

---

## What to Track

### Trade Log

| Field | What to Record |
|-------|----------------|
| Date/Time | Exact entry |
| Setup | What triggered entry |
| Entry Price | Exact price |
| Exit Price | Exact price |
| P&L | Profit or loss |
| Reason for trade | Setup description |
| Emotion | How you felt |

---

### Emotional Log

| Question | Record |
|----------|--------|
| Before trade | Emotional state |
| During trade | What you felt |
| After trade | Emotional outcome |

---

### Review Log

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| Did I follow rules? | Yes/No |
| What went right? | Specifics |
| What went wrong? | Specifics |
| What to adjust? | Action item |

---

## Journal Template

```
Date: _________
Pair: _________
Setup: _________
Entry: _________
Exit: _________
P&L: _________
Emotion: _________
Rules followed: Yes/No
Notes: _________
```

---

## How to Review

### Weekly Review

- Review all trades
- Identify patterns
- Adjust if needed

### Monthly Review

- Track win rate
- Track P&L
- Track emotional patterns
- Adjust strategy if needed

---

## What Journal Shows

| Pattern | What It Means |
|---------|------------|
| Lose after winning | Complacency |
| Lose after losing | Revenge |
| No setup trades | Patience |
| Multiple losses | Wrong market |

---

# Key Takeaways

1. **Track everything** — Every trade
2. **Review weekly** — Pattern check
3. **Adjust quarterly** — Strategy review
4. **Emotional log** — Know yourself
