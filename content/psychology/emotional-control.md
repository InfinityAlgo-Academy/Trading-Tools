# Emotional Control

Emotion in trading leads to bad decisions. Control your emotions, or they will control you.

---

## Core Emotions

### Fear

| Fear Type | Response |
|-----------|--------|
| Fear of losing | Accept it as cost of trading |
| Fear of missing out | Wait for your setup |
| Fear of being wrong | Expect some wrong trades |

### Greed

| Greed Type | Response |
|-----------|--------|
| Wanting more | Take profits as planned |
| Over-trading | Stick to plan |
| Increasing size too fast | Follow sizing rules |

---

## Emotional Control Techniques

### 1. Pre-Trade Check

Before every trade:

```
☐ Am I emotional?
☐ Is this my setup?
☐ Is risk defined?
☐ Do I accept potential loss?
```

If any "no" → Don't trade.

---

### 2. Breathing Technique

When feeling emotional:

```
1. Step back
2. Take 3 deep breaths
3. Reassess
4. Continue only if calm
```

---

### 3. Time-Based Rule

When emotionally charged:

```
Wait 1 hour before trading
Walk away
Review rules
Return only if calm
```

---

## Emotional States

| State | Trading Fitness |
|-------|----------------|
| Calm | ✅ Trade |
| Mild anxiety | ⚠️ Caution |
| Angry | ❌ Don't trade |
| FOMO | ❌ Don't trade |
| Revengeful | ❌ Don't trade |

---

## Common Mistakes

### 1. Trading While Emotional

**Problem:** Trading angry or frustrated.

**Fix:** Stop. Walk away.

---

### 2. Ignoring Emotions

**Problem:** Not recognizing feelings.

**Fix:** Check emotions before trade.

---

# Key Takeaways

1. **Check情绪** — Before trading
2. **Stop if emotional** — Don't force it
3. **Use technique** — Breathing, time
4. **Accept variance** — Some will lose
