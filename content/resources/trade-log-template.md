## Template Summary

### Entry Details
- Date/Time: When entered
- Pair: What traded
- Setup: What was setup
- Direction: Long or Short
- Entry Price: Exact entry
- Trigger: What triggered entry

### Management Details
- Stop Loss: Where placed
- Target: Where target set
- Size: Position size
- Risk: Dollar risk

### Exit Details  
- Exit Price: Actually exited
- Reason: Why exited
- P&L: Profit or loss

### Analysis Details
- Rules: Did you follow?
- Emotions: How felt?
- Quality: Rate 1-5 each
