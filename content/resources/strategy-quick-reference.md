# Strategy Quick Reference

## Strategy Categories

### 1. Trend Following

| Setup | Entry | Stop | Target |
|-------|-------|------|--------|
| Pullback to SMA | Price bounces | Below swing low | New high |
| MA crossover | Cross above | Below cross | Follow trend |
| Breakout | Above high | Below low | Height of move |

### 2. Mean Reversion

| Setup | Entry | Stop | Target |
|-------|-------|------|--------|
| RSI extreme | RSI < 30 | Below low | Middle |
| Bollinger bounce | Lower band touch | Below band | Middle band |
| VWAP | Below VWAP | Below VWAP | Above VWAP |

### 3. Breakout Trading

| Setup | Entry | Stop | Target |
|-------|-------|------|--------|
| Range break | Above/below | Inside range | Height |
| Trendline | Break + confirm | Beyond line | Structure |
| Support break | Below + confirm | Above | Height |

### 4. Range Trading

| Setup | Entry | Stop | Target |
|-------|-------|------|--------|
| Support | At support | Below support | Resistance |
| Resistance | At resistance | Above resistance | Support |

## Strategy Selection Guide

| Market Condition | Strategy |
|-------------------|----------|
| Strong trend | Trend following |
| No trend | Range trading |
| Low volatility | Breakout |
| Extreme reading | Mean reversion |
