# How This Site Works

This isn't just a collection of articles. It's a structured learning path designed to take you from beginner to profitable trader.

---

## The Structure

This site is organized in sequential phases:

```
start-here/   → Your starting point
foundations/  → Core concepts
markets/      → Understanding markets
tools/       → Set up your workspace
analysis/    → Technical analysis
strategies/  → Build your system
risk-management/ → Protect your capital
psychology/  → Master your mindset
advanced/   → Elite techniques
practice/   → Apply what you learn
resources/  → Tools and references
case-studies/ → Real examples
```

Each folder builds on the previous.

## How to Study

### Don't Just Read

1. **Read** the material
2. **Practice** on a demo account
3. **Journal** your trades
4. **Review** your performance

### One Thing at a Time

- Complete one section before moving to the next
- Master each concept before adding complexity
- Build your knowledge layer by layer

### Keep a Trading Journal

Every trader needs a journal. Track:

- What you learned
- What you practiced
- What worked and didn't
