# Liquidity Zones

Liquidity is where the orders are. Find liquidity pools, and you find where price will go.

---

## What Is Liquidity?

```
Liquidity = Clustered orders waiting to be filled

Above recent highs = Stop liquidity (sell orders)
Below recent lows = Stop liquidity (buy orders)
```

**Price moves toward liquidity, triggers it, then reverses.**

---

## Types of Liquidity

### 1. Stop Liquidity

| Location | Meaning | What Happens |
|----------|---------|------------|
| Above highs | Many stops | Price spikes, stops triggered, reverses |
| Below lows | Many stops | Price drops, stops triggered, reverses |

### 2. Limit Order Liquidity

| Location | Meaning | What Happens |
|----------|---------|------------|
| Support | Buy orders pending | Price bounces |
| Resistance | Sell orders pending | Price rejects |

### 3. Market Structure Liquidity

| Type | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| Recent highs | Past buying |
| Recent lows | Past selling |
| Swing points | Order clusters |

---

## Finding Liquidity Zones

### Method 1: Recent Highs/Lows

```
Recent swing high = Above stop liquidity
Recent swing low = Below stop liquidity
```

### Method 2: Round Numbers

```
1.1000, 1.2000, 1.3000
50.00, 100.00
$1,000, $2,000
```

Round numbers magnet price.

### Method 3: Range Edges

```
Range high = Resistance liquidity
Range low = Support liquidity
```

---

## Liquidity Trading Strategies

### Strategy 1: Liquidity Run

```
1. Price approaches high liquidity
2. Spikes into stops
3. Reverses with momentum
4. Entry: Opposite way
```

### Strategy 2: Liquidity Magnet

```
1. Price moving toward round number
2. Slows near number
3. Reverses on touch
4. Entry: On rejection
```

### Strategy 3: Liquidity Grab

```
1. Identified liquidity zone
2. Price reaches zone
3. Absorbs (doesn't reverse)
4. Breakout: Follow through
```

## Common Mistakes

### 1. Trading Into Unknown Liquidity

**Problem:** Not identifying where liquidity is.

**Solution:** Map liquidity zones first.

---

### 2. No Confirmation

**Problem:** Entering before reversal.

**Solution:** Wait for price action.

---

### 3. Fighting Strong Trends

**Problem:** Looking for liquidity reversal in strong trend.

**Solution:** Follow trend, not against.

---

# Key Takeaways

1. **Liquidity = Orders** — Clustered orders
2. **Price seeks liquidity** — Moves toward it
3. **Stop hunting** — Runs stops, reverses
4. **Round numbers** — Magnet price
5. **Map zones first** — Before trading
