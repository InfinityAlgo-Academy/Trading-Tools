# Trend Following Strategy

Trade with trend. Let winners run.

---

## Trend Definition

| Trend | Structure |
|-------|-----------|
| Uptrend | Higher highs, higher lows |
| Downtrend | Lower highs, lower lows |

---

## Entry Types

### 1. Breakout Entry

- New high in uptrend → Enter

### 2. Pullback Entry

- Price returns to key support → Enter

### 3. Moving Average Entry

- Price pulls back to 20 EMA → Enter

---

## Trend Following Rules

### Entry

```
1. Trend up (HH, HL)
2. Price at support level
3. Signal to enter
4. BUY
```

### Exit

```
1. Lower low forms (downtrend)
2. Stop hits
3. SELL
```

---

## Stop Strategy

| Method | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Swing low | Below recent low |
| ATR | Below recent low + ATR |
| Trailing | Trail below higher lows |

---

## Common Mistakes

### 1. Fighting Trend

**Fix:** Trade with trend always

### 2. Early Exit

**Fix:** Trail stop, let profits run

---

# Key Takeaways

1. **With trend** — Always
2. **Let winners run** — Trail, don't exit
3. **Cut losers** — Stop hits, exit
4. **Simple** — HH/HL confirms
