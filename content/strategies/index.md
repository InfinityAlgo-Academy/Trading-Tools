## Popular Strategies

### [[strategies/Trend-Following]]

Trade with the trend. Higher probability setups.

- Entry: Pullback to moving average
- Stop: Below swing low
- Target: New high
- Best for: Trending markets

---

### [[strategies/Breakout-Strategy]]

Trade momentum breakouts.

- Entry: Close beyond level
- Stop: Below breakout level
- Target: Pattern height
- Best for: Volatile markets

---

### [[strategies/SMC]] (Smart Money Concepts)

Trade with institutional flow.

- Entry: Order block retest
- Stop: Below block
- Target: Next structure
- Best for: All markets

---

### [[strategies/Range-Trading-Strategy]]

Trade between support and resistance.

- Entry: Support/Resistance test
- Stop: Outside range
- Target: Opposite boundary
- Best for: Ranging markets

---

### [[strategies/RSI-Strategy]]

Mean reversion using RSI.

- Entry: RSI < 30 or > 70
- Stop: Beyond extreme
- Target: Middle
- Best for: Reversals

---

### [[strategies/MACD-Strategy]]

Momentum crossovers.

- Entry: MACD cross
- Stop: Below cross
- Target: Trend continuation
- Best for: Trend momentum
