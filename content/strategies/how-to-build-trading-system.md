# How to Build a Trading System: Complete Guide

A trading system is your business plan. Without one, you're gambling. Here's how to build one from scratch.

---

## What is a Trading System?

A trading system is a complete set of rules that defines:

- **When to enter** (entry rules)
- **When to exit** (exit rules)  
- **How much to risk** (risk rules)
- **What to trade** (market filter)

Without these four components, you're not trading - you're gambling.

---

## Step 1: Choose Your Market

### Options

| Market | Difficulty | Best For |
|--------|------------|---------|
| Forex | Medium | Beginners |
| Crypto | High | Risk-takers |
| Indices | Low | Beginners |

### Recommendation

Start with **one market**. Master it before expanding.

---

## Step 2: Choose Your Timeframe

### Timeframe Selection Guide

| Your Goal | Use Timeframe |
|-----------|---------------|
| Long-term | Weekly, Monthly |
| Swing trade | Daily, 4H |
| Day trade | 1H, 15M |
| Scalp | 15M, 5M |

### Recommendation

Start with **Daily or 4H** - Good balance of signals and stress.

---

## Step 3: Define Your Entry Rules

### Must Answer:

1. **What triggers entry?** (specific signal)
2. **What confirms it?** (confirmation)
3. **What's required?** (conditions)

### Example Entry Rules

```
Entry:
- Daily chart in Uptrend (price above 50 SMA)
- Price pulls back to 50 SMA
- Bounce with bullish candle
- Entry: On close above bounce candle
```

---

## Step 4: Define Your Exit Rules

### Must Answer:

1. **Where does stop go?**
2. **Where's target?**
3. **When to exit early?**

### Example Exit Rules

```
Stop:
- Below bounce low

Target:
- Previous high, or
- 2:1 reward:risk

Early exit:
- RSI at 70 + reversal candle
```

---

## Step 5: Define Risk Rules

### Must Answer:

1. **What's max risk per trade?**
2. **What's max daily risk?**
3. **What's max concurrent positions?**

### Example Risk Rules

```
Max risk: 2% per trade
Max daily: 6%
Max positions: 3
Loss limit: Stop trading at 6% daily
```

## Step 7: Backtest Your System

### Backtest Process

1. **Manual test** - At least 50 trades
2. **Record results** - Every trade
3. **Calculate metrics** - Win rate, PF, DD
4. **Adjust** - If needed

### Required Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Win Rate | 40%+ |
| Profit Factor | 1.5+ |
| Max Drawdown | <15% |

---

## Step 8: Demo Test

### Demo Trading Rules

- Same rules as live
- Same position sizes
- Minimum 20-30 trades
- Track EVERY trade

---

## Step 9: Go Live

### Starting Rules

1. **Start tiny** - 25% of planned size
2. **Track everything** - Journal every trade
3. **Review weekly** - Adjust if needed
4. **Only size up** - After consistent profits

## Common System Building Mistakes

### 1. Overcomplicating

**Problem:** Too many rules

**Solution:** Keep simple, 3-5 rules max

---

### 2. Curve Fitting

**Problem:** Optimizing too much on backtest

**Solution:** Use robust settings, not perfect past results

---

### 3. No Written Rules

**Problem:** In head only

**Solution:** Always write down
