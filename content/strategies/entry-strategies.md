# Entry Strategies

Entry is when your analysis meets action. Define entry rules precisely.

---

## Entry Types

### 1. Breakout Entry

Enter when price breaks a level.

**Setup:**
- Consolidation forms
- Price breaks high
- Enter on close above

**When to use:**
- Strong momentum
- Clear levels

---

### 2. Pullback Entry

Enter on pullback to support.

**Setup:**
- Trend established
- Price retests level
- Entry on bounce

**When to use:**
- Trend continuation
- Better risk/reward

---

### 3. Retest Entry

Enter on retest of breakout.

**Setup:**
- Breakout occurs
- Price returns to level
- Entry on retest hold

**When to use:**
- After false breakouts

---

### 4. Signal Entry

Enter on indicator signal.

**Setup:**
- RSI crosses up
- MACD crosses
- Entry on signal

**When to use:**
- With structure confirmed

---

## Entry Checklist

```
☐ Setup identified
☐ Level marked
☐ Confirm price action
☐ Entry trigger fired
☐ Stop placed
☐ Target defined
```

---

# Key Takeaways

1. **Define rules** — Before entering
2. **Wait for confirmation** — Don't anticipate
3. **Match to strategy** — Different strokes
4. **Always stop** — Never entry without stop
