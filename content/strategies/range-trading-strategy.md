# Range Trading Strategy

Trade within defined support and resistance.

---

## What Is Range Trading?

Price moves between support and resistance. Buy low, sell high.

---

## Range Types

### 1. Horizontal Range

```

     ╲    ╱
```

---

## Range Trading Rules

### Buy

```
1. Price at support
2. Signal to buy
3. BUY
```

### Sell

```
1. Price at resistance
2. Signal to sell
3. SELL
```

---

## Stop & Target

| Element | Value |
|---------|-------|
| Buy stop | Below support |
| Sell stop | Above resistance |
| Buy target | Resistance |
| Sell target | Support |

---

## Common Mistakes

### 1. Fighting Breakout

**Fix:** Exit when range breaks

### 2. No Confirmation

**Fix:** Wait for signals

---

# Key Takeaways

1. **Buy support** — Sell resistance
2. **Exit breakout** — When range breaks
3. **Signal entry** — Wait for confirmation
4. **Clear levels** — Define range
