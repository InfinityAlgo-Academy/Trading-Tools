# MACD Strategy

Trade using MACD crossovers and divergences.

---

## Setup

### Components

- MACD (12, 26, 9)
- Support/resistance
- Trend identification

### Timeframe

- Daily for swing
- 4H for intraday

---

## Rules

### Long Entry

```
1. Trend up or ranging
2. MACD crosses above signal
3. Price at support/confluence
4. Confirmation → BUY
```

### Short Entry

```
1. Trend down or ranging
2. MACD crosses below signal
3. Price at resistance/confluence
4. Confirmation → SELL
```

---

## Stop & Target

| Element | Value |
|---------|-------|
| Stop | Below/above recent swing |
| Target | 2:1 or 3:1 |

---

## Common Mistakes

### 1. Trading Every Crossover

**Fix:** Only trade with trend

### 2. No Stop

**Fix:** Always have stop

---

# Key Takeaways

1. **MACD** — Momentum indicator
2. **Crossover** — Signal
3. **With trend** — Higher probability
4. **Confirm** — With price
