# Price Action

Trading without indicators. Pure price movement tells you everything.

**Core principle:** The chart reflects all buying and selling. Read it.

---

## What Is Price Action?

Price action is reading raw price movement on charts — no indicators, no algorithms.

**Why it works:**
- Price = truth
- Indicators lag
- Price moves first, indicators confirm

---

## Core Price Action Concepts

### Swing Highs and Lows

| Pattern | Meaning |
|---------|----------|
| Higher high | Buyers in control |
| Lower high | Selling pressure |
| Higher low | Buyers stepping in |
| Lower low | Sellers in control |

---

### Support and Resistance

| Level | Meaning |
|-------|----------|
| Support | Buying pressure |
| Resistance | Selling pressure |

**Key:** Stronger with each test.

---

### Trend Identification

| Trend | Structure |
|-------|-----------|
| Uptrend | HH, HL |
| Downtrend | LH, LL |
| Range | Bounded |

## Price Action Trading

### Step 1: Identify Structure

- Trend or range?
- What's the bias?

---

### Step 2: Mark Key Levels

- Recent highs
- Recent lows
- Pivot points

---

### Step 3: Wait for Setup

| Setup | Trigger |
|-------|----------|
| Support bounce | Price tests support |
| Resistance rejection | Price hits resistance |
| Breakout | Price breaks structure |

---

### Step 4: Enter

- On confirmation
- With stop

## Common Patterns

### Pin Bar (Pinocchio)

```
       Wick
        ↗
      ╱   ↘
     ╱     ╲
    ╱       ↘
          Body
```

**Why it works:** Price rejected from level.

**Trade:** Enter at close of pin bar.

---

### Engulfing

```
Bearish Engulfing:
    ╲    ╱
     ╲  ╱
      ╲╱
     ╱╲
    ╱  ╲
   ╱    ↘
```

**Why it works:** Momentum shift.

**Trade:** Enter on close of engulfing.

---

### Inside Bar

```
   Inside
    ┌─┐
    │█│
    └─┘
```

**Why it works:** Consolidation before break.

**Trade:** Breakout of inside bar.

---

## Price Action vs Indicators

| Aspect | Price Action | Indicators |
|--------|-------------|------------|
| Speed | Immediate | Lagging |
| Objective | Somewhat | Highly |
| Reliable | In trends | In ranges |

---

## Common Mistakes

### 1. Subjectivity

**Problem:** Everyone reads charts differently.

**Fix:** Define clear rules.

---

### 2. No Confirmation

**Problem:** Entering on potential.

**Fix:** Wait for confirmation.

---

### 3. Ignoring Context

**Problem:** Trading pin bars in ranges.

**Fix:** Trade in trend direction.
